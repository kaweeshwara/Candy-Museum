EXPERIENCE THE MAGIC OF CANDY

Welcome to the Museum of Candy, where every corner is filled with delicious history and sweet surprises. Dive into the world of candy, discover iconic treats, and learn how they’ve shaped the world. Whether you’re a candy connoisseur or a casual sweet-tooth, there’s something here for everyone!

Explore interactive exhibits, candy-making workshops, and unique candy collections. Don’t forget to grab your favorite sweets at our candy shop before you leave!
